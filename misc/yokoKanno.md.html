Songs that sound like Yoko Kanno

Yoko Kanno is an artist that makes music for a lot of anime. A lot of their music sounds like music by other artists, but I haven't seen anyone try to compile a list. The good news is that if you're a fan of Yoko Kanno's work there's a whole lot of very similar stuff out there.

There are a lot of these, shoot me an email if you know more and I'll add them.
